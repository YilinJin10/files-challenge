Olaf

Olaf is a snowman character from the 2013 animated film Frozen, produced by Walt Disney Animation Studios.


His first appearance in Frozen is during Anna and Elsa's childhood as an inanimate snowman. Later, Olaf is recreated during the first verse of "Let It Go", and unknown to Elsa, comes to life. A few days later, Anna and Kristoff cross paths with Olaf while traveling through a meadow. Anna's first instinct is to give Olaf the carrot nose that he had been missing. Before they continue on their way, Olaf sings a shot number fantasizing about what it might be like to experience summer heat, and blissfully unaware that he'll melt. Olaf then guides Anna and Kristoff to Elsa's ice palace.
